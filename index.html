SOCIAL WORK


Our Staff Members and Volunteers try to feed as many dogs as possible in and around the city.
More than 1000 Members have joined our Organization as Volunteers and are ready to help us, spending their quality time.

OUR ORGANISATION

These are some Photos of our organization.
We make sure that every dog in our organization is healthy and fit.
Each and every Dog in our organization is vaccinated and is free from any diseases.

  • Every day around 1000-1200 street dogs are fed daiy by our Organisation.
  • Each day, around 20 - 30 street dogs which live in our Organisation, are adopted and get their new owners.
  • Every Rehoming center has a Veteran Doctor, which takes care of Inhome dogs as well as takes care of Home Pet dogs on special Request.
  • Each Day new 30 - 40 dogs are Adopted by our Organisation, so that they can be trained well for Homely pet.
